About

After a failed city contract, a local security company in Chicago needed a fresh approach in responding to an RFP. They wanted to competively bid on a major airport contract but lacked the necessary resources. After noticing their need for proposal management, we assisted with managing their bid response from start to finish. Small business owners often need skilled writers and editors to strenthen their proposal response.

Murry Consultancy helps small to medium sized business' succeed in their proposal process from start through finalist selection. We also cover grant writing, government, and other RFP's.
